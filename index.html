Who's my user?

Edie, 46, lives in SF Married, kids in college Household income > 300K HR Consultant (Flexible Schedule, Home Office + In house Consultations —> spends a lot of time traveling/commuting) House in SF Just bought a second home in Point Reyes, and is looking to decorate. She is mature and knows what she likes, but is does not necessarily know where to find Husband is busy, works long hours. Is a CD at an ad firm. He has opinions and taste, but is not the primary decision maker on this project. OKL: she is familiar with the brand, has bought one or two small items in the past, but the couple bought their first home more than 10 years ago, and OKL didn’t exist.

Her Needs?

She wants to have a place where she can collect ideas for furniture and home decor pieces for her 2nd home. compare ideas for furniture and home decor pieces, find inspiration, easily share with her husband. She should be able to do this at any time, and anywhere: she has free time when she is Ubering from client to client, so it’s important that the experience be mobile friendly and easily accessible on the go. Help her choose! Get advice from designer get friend/spouse/decorator’s opinion
